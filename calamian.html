Calamian Deer

Calamian deer (Axis calamianensis) has short legs, short stocky legs , and slightly raised hindquarters. The underparts are paler than the rest of the body, which is uniformly golden-brown. Dark brown (nearly black in some species) characterizes the legs, and a dark dorsal stripe runs from the shoulders to the bushy tail's base. White lips with dark bars encircling them. There is a little, whitish "bib" on the upper throat. The inner surfaces of the ears are mostly white and are somewhat rounded.
