over time due to several factors, including natural disasters, pollution, habitat destruction, and destructive human activities.
To prevent their extinction, we must safeguard the Philippines' wild creatures.

This blog shows the current list of endangered creatures
in the Philippines would perish if we do not act to save and protect them.
