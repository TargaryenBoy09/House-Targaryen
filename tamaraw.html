PHILIPPINE ENDANGERED SPECIES

Tamaraw

Tamaraws (Bulbalus mindorensis) also known as mindoro dwarf buffalos. They are herbivores and mostly consume grasses, bamboo shoots and wild sugar cane. Tamaraws have been subjected to hunting for food and entertainment purposes in the past, which is why they are critically endangered.
