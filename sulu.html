PHILIPPINE ENDANGERED SPECIES

Sulu hornbill

Sulu hornbill (Anthracoceros montani) is a type of hornbill that belongs to the Bucerotidae family. It is an endangered species thought to have been hunted to extinction on Jolo. Tropical damp forests are its natural habitat.
