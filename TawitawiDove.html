PHILIPPINE ENDANGERED SPECIES

Tawitawi Brown Dove

Tawitawi brown dove (Phapitreron cinereiceps) is an endangered species of bird in the Columbidae family distinguished by its orange-peach breast. It most likely has a very small population and has had a very quick population drop due to a reduction in the size of its forest habitat, which is currently only found in a few small, remnant portions spread across a very short area. As a result, it is listed as endangered.
