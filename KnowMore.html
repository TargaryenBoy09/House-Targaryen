SAINT LOUIS
UNIVERSITY

Saint Louis University, also referred to by its acronym SLU, is a
private Catholic research basic and higher education institution
run by the Congregation of the Immaculate Heart of Mary in
Baguio City, Benguet Philippines. It was founded on
December 1, 1911, by the CICM Missionaries.

NAVY BASE CAMPUS

Navy Base, St. Joseph Village is home to Navy Base Campus, a 5-hectare (12-acre) facility.

MAIN CAMPUS

The 6-hectare (15-acre) Main Campus is located in Area A. Bonifacio Street. The SAS School, commonly known as the School of Advanced Studies, is located on Main Campus. This is where the School of Engineering and Architecture, or SEA, is located. There is also a School of Law (SOL) in this area. The School of Nursing, Allied Health, and Biological Sciences, or SONAHBS, is situated here. Here you can find the SOM, also referred to as the School of Medicine. This is where you'll find STELA, or the School of Teacher Education and Liberal Arts.

MARYHEIGHTS CAMPUS

An 8-hectare (20-acre) property in Brgy is called Maryheights Campus. Bakakeng. The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing, and Information Studies, or SAMCIS for short, is located on the Maryheights Campus.

GONZAGA CAMPUS

The 12-acre (5-hectare) Gonzaga Campus is located on General Luna Street in Baguio City. The School of BEdS, often known as the SLU Basic Education School, is located on Gonzaga Campus.
